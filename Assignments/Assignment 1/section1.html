Question 5

My favorite Disney movies are:

  1. Aladdin
    • Character development
    • The music with hits like "A Whole New World"
    • The film’s vibrant "Arabian Nights" aesthetic
  2. The Lion King
    • Powerful coming-of-age tale about responsibility, grief, and finding your place in the "Circle of Life."
    • Great background score, creates an emotional depth
    • Memorable Villains & Sidekicks
  3. Zootopia
    • Beneath the bright colors and cute animals is a sophisticated look at prejudice, stereotypes, and how fear can be used to manipulate a society.
    • The chemistry between the optimistic Judy Hopps and the cynical Nick Wilde creates a fantastic mystery-solving duo that keeps the plot moving at a fast pace.
    • The city of Zootopia is incredibly detailed, with different districts (like Tundratown and Sahara Square) designed for different species, making the world feel lived-in and real.
  4. Tangled
    • Memorable characters
    • Stunning animation especially the "Lantern Scene"
    • Great story with a relatable heroine
  5. Frozen
    • Sisterly love triumphs over romantic love
    • Memorable songs like "Let It Go", seeing it in the context of Elsa’s journey toward self-acceptance remains a powerful cinematic moment.
    • A witty sense of humor
